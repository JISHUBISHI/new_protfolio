My Journey

My passion for building projects started when I was in First Year of my B.Tech, tinkering with basic programming concepts. What began as simple scripts soon evolved into complex applications as my curiosity grew.

I remember staying up late nights debugging my first AI model, a simple image classifier. The moment it correctly identified a cat image after countless failures was magical. That's when I knew I wanted to pursue AI seriously.

Since then, I've built various projects that solve real-world problems, from voice assistants to legal document analyzers. Each project teaches me something new and pushes me to learn more. The thrill of turning an idea into a working product is what keeps me coding.
